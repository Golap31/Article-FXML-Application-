# Article-FXML-Application-
A research article hub where information of various published articles will be stored.
Each
research article has the following information:
● Title - Name of the article. This title must be unique.
● Author - Name of the first author of the article
● Article Type - Must be one of the following: “Review”, “Book”, “Case”.
● Publication Date - The date when this article was published.
● Citation Status - Must be one of the following: “Yes”, “No”. Use a checkbox for this field.
● No of Authors - Number of authors of the article.
